what is margheritas and margaritas?

margheritas & margaritas is the small-menu-big-flavour restaurant you didn’t know you needed.
it’s just pizzas and cocktails, cocktails and pizzas, margheritas and margaritas.
born from a love for simple, high quality food and drink - we don't do much, but we do it right.

your friends invite you out for margs.
‘which kind?’, you ask.
‘both’.

my name is olivia, but you can call me miss margherita, miss margarita, or oli. i like to drink cocktails, i like to eat pizza and i love to cook. i am also a junior web developer.

if you hadn't guessed by now, margheritas & margaritas is an imaginary concept restaurant.
instead of selling pizzas and cocktails, this website is a project designed to sell my web design and development skills.

you can see more of what i get up to on my github. thanks for visiting!
